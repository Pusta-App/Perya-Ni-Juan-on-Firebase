COLOR GAME

HOW TO PLAY

1. Choose your bet amount just by cliking on the red arrow to navigate into the list of bills available. 2. Place your bet just by tapping on the color provided namely red, green, blue, pink, orange, and yellow. 3. If your sure about your bet placed, you can now pull the lever down located in the top right side of the screen. 4. The cubes will be release and result will be displayed. After double checking for the result you can now tap on the reset button to play again. PAYOUT ODDS: x1 Color - 1:1 , x2 Color - 1:2 , and x3 Color - 1:3.

BALL DROP

HOW TO PLAY

Pula Puti is played using three (3) ping-pong balls thrown in any way inside a transparent funnel by the player with the highest bet or his designated thrower. The balls fall through the funnel to settle onto three (3) compartments on a 100-square checkerboard. The checkerboard consists of forty-nine (49) WHITE squares, forty-nine (49) RED squares and two (2) BLUE squares bearing a STAR symbol. The final color on which at least two (2) ping-pong balls come to rest shall be considered the winning color. If any of the three ping-pong balls rest on a STAR, bets on RED and WHITE lose. PAYOUT ODDS are: x2 Red - 1:1, x2White - 1:1, x3 Red - 1:7, x3 White - 1:7, x1 blue w/ star - 1:12, and x2 blue w/ star - 1:490.

MANOK ROLETA

HOW TO PLAY

Sabong Roulette is played using a rotating flat wheel commonly made up of circular wood with nail arround. Inside the circle, space are devided into 12 parts having a specific value, value are sometimes color, numbers, cards, or even cock fighting types. This game is similar to what is commonly known as 'Wheel of Fortune' game yet with a little bit of twist, anyone can play it at the same time. PAYOUT ODDS are: 1:1 (If you win, the banker will return your bet and win amount equal to your bet amount.)
